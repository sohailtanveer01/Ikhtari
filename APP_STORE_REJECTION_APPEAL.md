## 📝 Appeal Response Template

Use this response when replying to Apple in App Store Connect:

---

**Subject:** Appeal - Guideline 4.3(b) - Unique Features Justification

**Body:**

Dear App Review Team,

Thank you for your review. We respectfully request reconsideration as Habibi Swipe offers significant unique features and functionality that differentiate it from other dating apps in the App Store.

**Our Unique Differentiators:**

1. **Halal Content Moderation System:** Our app implements real-time content filtering for both Arabic and English languages, blocking inappropriate content that violates Islamic values. This dual-language, culturally-specific moderation system is unique to our platform and essential for our Muslim user base. We actively filter profanity, sexual content, and harassment in both languages to maintain a respectful, Halal environment.

2. **Compliments Feature:** Unlike standard dating apps where you can only swipe, Habibi Swipe allows users to send meaningful compliments before matching. These compliments can lead to instant matches when accepted, providing an alternative way to express genuine interest beyond passive swiping. This feature is fully implemented with accept/decline functionality.

3. **Compliments Feature:** Users can send compliments before matching, which can lead to instant matches. This pre-match communication system provides an alternative to passive swiping and allows users to express genuine interest through meaningful messages.

4. **Comprehensive Religiosity Matching:** Unlike apps with basic religion filters, Habibi Swipe offers detailed Islamic-specific matching including:
   - Sect preferences (Sunni, Shia, Sufi, etc.)
   - Religious practice level (actively practicing, moderately practicing, not practicing)
   - Born Muslim vs. Convert preference
   
   This depth of religious compatibility matching is unique to Muslim-focused platforms.

5. **Marriage-Focused Positioning:** Habibi Swipe is explicitly designed for serious relationships leading to marriage (Niqah), not casual dating. Our messaging, features, and community guidelines all reflect this commitment to marriage-oriented connections.

6. **Islamic Cultural Filters:** Our filtering system includes lifestyle preferences tailored to Muslim values, including alcohol habits (with Islamic context), marital status considerations (divorced, widowed - culturally relevant options), and family-oriented preferences.

7. **Rematch Capability:** Users can request rematches with previously unmatched users, reflecting Islamic values of second chances and forgiveness - a feature not commonly found in mainstream dating apps. This is a fully implemented feature with request/accept/decline flows.

**Target Audience:**
Habibi Swipe serves a specific niche - the global Muslim community seeking marriage. While dating apps are common, Muslim-focused matrimony apps with these Islamic-specific features are limited and serve an underserved community.

**App Store Positioning:**
We position Habibi Swipe as a **matrimony and marriage-focused platform**, not a casual dating app. Our tagline "From a Swipe to Niqah" emphasizes our commitment to serious relationships.

**Community Need:**
The Muslim community has specific cultural and religious requirements for marriage that mainstream dating apps cannot adequately address. Habibi Swipe fills this gap with features built specifically for Islamic values and traditions.

We believe these unique features and our specific target audience justify Habibi Swipe's place in the App Store. We are open to adjusting our App Store listing to better highlight these differentiators.
