## 📝 Full App Description

```
Habibi Swipe - Find Your Perfect Match

Welcome to Habibi Swipe, the premier Muslim dating app designed to help you find your Habibi with respect, tradition, and modern technology. From a Swipe to Niqah - we're here to help you find your perfect match for marriage.

🌙 CORE FEATURES

Swipe & Discover:
• Swipe through profiles of verified Muslim singles in your area
• Intuitive swipe interface - swipe right to like, left to pass
• See who liked you in the "Liked Me" section
• View who has viewed your profile
• Browse profiles you've passed on

Smart Matching:
• Find matches based on shared values, religiosity, and lifestyle preferences
• Comprehensive filtering system to find your ideal partner:
  - Age range filter
  - Location-based matching (city, state, distance radius)
  - Religiosity preferences (sect, religious practice, born Muslim)
  - Ethnicity filter with search capability
  - Height preferences
  - Marital status preferences
  - Children preferences
• Advanced matching algorithm considers compatibility factors

Secure Messaging:
• Chat with your matches in a safe, Halal environment
• Send text messages and images
• Real-time messaging with read receipts
• Active status indicators
• Photo protection to prevent screenshots in chat
• Match expiration system - matches expire if no conversation within 24 hours

Compliments Feature:
• Send compliments to stand out and express genuine interest
• Receive compliments from other users
• Accept or decline compliments
• Compliments can lead to instant matches

Profile Features:
• Create detailed profiles showcasing your personality:
  - Multiple profile photos
  - Personal bio (up to 1000 characters)
  - Education and profession
  - Religious information and practices
  - Lifestyle preferences (alcohol, smoking)
  - Hobbies and interests
  - Custom profile prompts with answers
  - Height, marital status, and children information
• Edit your profile anytime
• Privacy controls for your information

💍 BUILT FOR SERIOUS RELATIONSHIPS

• Detailed profiles showcasing religion, values, and lifestyle
• Designed with marriage in mind, not casual dating
• Respectful environment that honors Islamic principles
• Community-driven platform built by Muslims, for Muslims
• Age verification ensures all users are 18+

🔒 PRIVACY & SAFETY

• Photo protection to prevent screenshots on sensitive screens
• Report and block users for a safe experience
• Secure authentication via email, Google, or Apple Sign-In
• All data transmitted securely over HTTPS/TLS encryption
• Privacy-focused design with user control over information sharing
• Account deletion available anytime

✨ PREMIUM FEATURES

• Unlock additional features with premium subscription
• Enhanced visibility and matching opportunities
• Access to premium filters and preferences

🌟 WHY HABIBI SWIPE?

• Community-driven platform built by Muslims, for Muslims
• Respectful environment that honors Islamic values
• Modern, intuitive interface that's easy to use
• Connect with like-minded individuals seeking meaningful relationships
• Comprehensive filtering to find your perfect match
• Safe and secure platform for serious connections
• Real-time messaging and active status updates
• Match expiration system encourages meaningful conversations

📱 EASY TO USE

• Simple sign-up process with email, Google, or Apple Sign-In
• Intuitive swipe interface
• Clean, modern design with gold and black theme
• Smooth navigation between features
```
